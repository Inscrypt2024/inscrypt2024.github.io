How to Register
1. Fill out the registration form for foreign attendees or registration form for attendees from Mainland China with all required details and email the filled form to Inscrypt2023@163.com with the subject of your email "Inscrypt 2023 Registration for #PAPER-ID(if applicable)#NAME#Work unit" (where PAPER-ID is the submission number of your paper and NAME is the author name).
